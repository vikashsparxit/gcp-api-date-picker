# Privacy Policy — GCP APIs: Custom Date & Request Total

**Last updated:** March 2026

This privacy policy describes how the Chrome extension **"GCP APIs: Custom Date & Request Total"** (the Extension) handles information when you use it.

## Summary

- The Extension **does not collect, sell, or share any personal data**.
- The Extension **does not send any data to external servers**. All processing happens in your browser.
- The Extension uses Chrome’s **storage** and **tabs** permissions only as described below.

---

## Data the Extension Uses

### 1. Chrome Storage (`storage` permission)

The Extension uses Chrome’s local storage to **save your last-selected date range** (start date and end date) in the date picker. This is stored only on your device so that when you reopen the extension, it can show your previous selection. This data is not sent anywhere and is not personally identifiable.

### 2. Tabs (`tabs` permission)

The Extension uses the tabs permission to:

- **Read the active tab’s URL** — Only to check whether you are on the Google Cloud Console APIs & Services dashboard (`console.cloud.google.com/apis/dashboard`). This determines whether the extension shows the date picker and total requests or a message to open the dashboard. The Extension does not read page content or any other tab data.
- **Update the active tab’s URL** — When you click “Apply Date Range,” the Extension updates the current tab’s URL to apply the selected date range on the GCP dashboard. This only affects the tab you are using; no URL or browsing data is sent to any server.

The Extension does not collect, store, or transmit your browsing history, tab titles, or any other tab information beyond what is needed for the above behavior.

---

## What We Do Not Do

- We do **not** collect personal or sensitive user data.
- We do **not** send any data to external servers or third parties.
- We do **not** use analytics, tracking, or advertising.
- We do **not** access the content of your emails, documents, or other Google account data. The Extension only interacts with the GCP APIs dashboard page when you choose to use it.

---

## Data Retention

- **Storage data** (your last date range) is stored locally in your browser. You can remove it by uninstalling the Extension or clearing the extension’s storage.
- The Extension does not retain any data on any server.

---

## Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the “Last updated” date at the top will be changed. Continued use of the Extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
